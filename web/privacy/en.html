2. Personal data we collect

For the game to work we collect and store the following data:

Category Data Source
Identification Email address Provided by the user at registration
Credentials Password hash (bcrypt) — never the plain-text password Derived from registration
Game profile Account UUID, pibe name, chosen club, timestamps Generated by the game

3. Purpose of processing

We use the data solely to:

  • Create and maintain your player account.
  • Allow you to authenticate and reset your password.
  • Persist your progress (pibe, club, stats) between sessions.
  • Operate the service (anti-fraud, abuse prevention, security logs).
  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not use the data for behavioural advertising, marketing profiling, or share it with ad networks.

4. International data transfer

The App's servers (Nakama + PostgreSQL) are hosted on Railway, LATAM region (a provider with regional presence suitable for Argentina). Argentina considers Brazil, the United States and other jurisdictions to provide an adequate level of protection. The exact region is specified in the internal document AAIP-REGISTRATION.md and updates are notified accordingly.

Transactional email (password reset) may be delegated to a US-based provider (for example, Resend); in that case, the legal basis for the transfer is performance of the contract at your request (Law 25.326, art. 12).

5. Data retention

Your account data is kept while your account is active. When you delete your account from the game (Home → "Borrar mi cuenta") we remove your profile and associated data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe (typically up to 30 days because of backups). Technical logs rotate after 30 days.

6. Data-subject rights (ARCO + portability)

Pursuant to Law 25.326 (arts. 14, 16 and 27) you may exercise the following rights free of charge:

  • Access: know what data we keep about you.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Cancellation / erasure: delete your data (also available in-app).
  • Objection: object to a specific processing.

To exercise these rights, write to privacidad@barrabrava.com.ar from the email associated with the account. We reply within 10 calendar days (Law 25.326, art. 14).

If you consider your rights are not respected, you may file a complaint with the Agencia de Acceso a la Información Pública (AAIP): argentina.gob.ar/aaip.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures: TLS on all connections, bcrypt password hashing, server access controls, encrypted backups, API rate limiting. No system is 100% impenetrable; in the event of a notable incident we will notify affected users and the AAIP as required.

8. Children

The service is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected data from a child under 13 without valid parental authorisation, we will delete it without delay.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version. Material changes will be notified inside the app.
